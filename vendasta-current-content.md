## The Parts Nobody Tells You

**It's Not Passive Income**

You still need to:
- Sell the services to clients
- Onboard them properly
- Monitor performance
- Handle escalations
- Maintain relationships

**Estimate:** 20-30 hours/month for 10-20 clients

**The First Month Is Rough**

Learning curve:
- Understanding all 250+ products (overwhelming)
- Configuring white-label properly (technical)
- Building your first packages (decision paralysis)
- Training clients on the portal (time-consuming)

Budget 40-50 hours the first month just learning the platform.

**Not Every Product Is Great**

250+ products sounds amazing.

Reality: You'll use 10-20 regularly.

Some products are:
- Outdated
- Clunky interfaces
- Missing features
- Better alternatives exist

You'll need to test products before offering them to clients.

**Client Education Is Required**

Your clients won't understand:
- Why they need these services
- How to use the portal
- What the reports mean
- When to expect results

You'll spend time educating them. Budget for this.

**Support Tickets Still Come to You**

Vendasta handles technical support.

But your clients will ask YOU first:
- "How do I log in?"
- "What does this report mean?"
- "Can we change X setting?"

You're the first line of support. Vendasta is your backup.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Mistake 1: Offering Too Many Services**

Trying to resell all 250+ products = confusion.

**Fix:** Start with 5-10 core services. Add more later.

**Mistake 2: Pricing Too Low**

Charging $200/month because you're afraid to lose clients.

**Fix:** Minimum $500/month. If they can't afford that, they're not your client.

**Mistake 3: No Client Education**

Set up their account and ghost them.

**Fix:** Weekly check-ins first month, then monthly. Educate constantly.

**Mistake 4: Targeting Wrong Clients**

Trying to sell to startups or solopreneurs who can't afford $500/month.

**Fix:** Target established local businesses doing $500K+ annual revenue.

**Mistake 5: Not Using It Yourself First**

Selling services you've never tested.

**Fix:** Use Vendasta for your own agency for 30 days before pitching clients.
