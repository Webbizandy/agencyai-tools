### Typical Agency Workflow

**Week 1 with a new client:**

1. **Content Audit** (30 min) - Gather their FAQs, docs, website content, common questions
2. **Upload & Train** (10 min) - Drag everything into ChatBase, click train, wait 5 minutes
3. **Test & Refine** (20 min) - Ask it 20-30 questions, spot gaps, add clarifying content
4. **Brand It** (15 min) - Logo, colors, welcome message
5. **Deploy** (5 min) - Embed on their site or send standalone link

Total: ~90 minutes to launch.

**Ongoing:** First month, review conversations weekly (30 min). Spot where it's giving wonky answers. Add better content. After month one, it's just monthly maintenance.

## FAQs

**Can I really charge $500-$1,500/month for this?**

Yes. You're not charging for software—you're charging for service. Setup, training, content creation, ongoing optimization, support. ChatBase is the tool. Your expertise is the value.

**What if the bot gives wrong answers?**

Review conversations weekly (first month), spot failures, add/clarify content. Accuracy improves over time. After month one, review monthly.

**Can I use this for lead generation?**

Absolutely. Enable "Collect email before answering." Every visitor who asks = email captured + their question for follow-up.

**Do I need technical skills?**

No. If you can upload a file and copy/paste embed code, you can build this.

**How long does setup actually take?**

1-2 hours per client chatbot. Most time is organizing their content properly (garbage in = garbage out).

**Can clients tell it's AI?**

If you set it up right, no. It responds in your tone with your content. The white-label branding makes it look like a custom-built solution.

**What happens when I hit message limits?**

You can buy extra message credits ($12 per 1,000) or upgrade plans. Most agencies on Standard plan (12,000 messages) handle 5-10 moderate-traffic client sites comfortably.

## Limitations

**Free tier is useless**
Only 100 messages/month. Won't cover proper testing. You need a paid plan from day one.

**AppSumo deals have restrictions**
Users report locked features, limited AI models access. If you're serious, go monthly.

**"Unlimited" isn't really unlimited**
Marketing says unlimited links. There are limits buried in fine print. Check before committing.

**Can't handle complex workflows alone**
Answers questions brilliantly. But appointment booking? Payment processing? You'll need Calendly or Zapier integration.

**First month requires weekly reviews**
Not "set and forget." Expect 30 min/week reviewing conversations, fixing wonky answers, adding content.

**Better Alternative for Complex Workflows:** If you need appointment booking, payment processing, or multi-step workflows without external integrations, check out **Closebot** - built specifically for agencies needing more than Q&A chatbots.
