## What Is Jobber?

Jobber is a CRM and business management platform built specifically for small home service contractors.

Think of it as ServiceTitan Lite ‚Äî same core features (scheduling, invoicing, customer management) but simpler, cheaper, and faster to set up.

**Core functions:**
- **Scheduling & Dispatch:** Drag-and-drop calendar, route optimization, recurring job scheduling
- **Mobile App for Crews:** Clock in/out, view job details, collect payments, take photos
- **Customer Management:** CRM with notes, job history, automated follow-ups
- **Invoicing & Payments:** Generate invoices on-site, accept credit cards via mobile
- **Online Booking:** Customers can book and pay online (like Calendly for contractors)
- **Quoting:** Create and send estimates, track conversions
- **Reporting:** Revenue tracking, job profitability, customer lifetime value

**Who uses it?**
- Lawn care & landscaping (40% of Jobber's user base)
- Cleaning services (residential & commercial)
- Handyman & maintenance
- Pest control
- Pool service
- HVAC & plumbing (small shops under $300K/year)

**Who it's NOT for:**
- Large contractors doing $1M+/year (use ServiceTitan)
- General construction (use Buildertrend or Procore)
- Retail or non-field service businesses

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Jobber have a free trial?

Yes. **14-day free trial**, no credit card required. Full access to all features on the Grow plan.

### Can I use Jobber if I'm a solo contractor?

Yes. The Core plan ($29/mo) is perfect for solo operators. You get scheduling, invoicing, mobile app, and online booking.

### Does Jobber integrate with QuickBooks?

Yes. Two-way sync with QuickBooks Online. Invoices, payments, and customer data sync automatically.

### Can customers book and pay online?

Yes. Online booking is included on all plans. Customers can book, pay, and get confirmation via email/SMS.

### Does Jobber have route optimization?

Yes, but only on the Grow plan ($169/mo) and higher. Core and Connect plans don't include route optimization.

### Can I white-label Jobber?

Yes, but only through the Jobber Partner Program. You need to apply and be approved. Typically requires 5+ clients before they'll approve white-label access.

### Does Jobber work offline?

Partially. The mobile app can view scheduled jobs offline, but you need internet to sync data, process payments, or update job details.

## Limitations & Drawbacks (The Honest Take)

### 1. **No Built-In Call Tracking**

Unlike ServiceTitan and Housecall Pro, Jobber doesn't have call tracking built-in. If you want to know which marketing channels drive phone calls, you need to use a 3rd-party tool like CallRail ($45/mo+).

**Workaround:** Use UTM parameters on web leads and manually ask phone callers "How did you hear about us?"

---

### 2. **Route Optimization Locked Behind $169/Mo Plan**

Route optimization is a HUGE time saver, but it's only available on Grow ($169/mo) or Manage ($349/mo). If you're on Core ($29/mo) or Connect ($79/mo), you're planning routes manually.

**Reality check:** If you have 2+ crews, pay for Grow. You'll save enough in gas and time to cover the cost.

---

### 3. **Limited Marketing Automation**

Jobber has basic automated emails (appointment reminders, follow-ups), but it's not a full marketing platform. No segmentation, no A/B testing, no advanced workflows.

**For agencies:** If you need sophisticated email marketing, connect Jobber to ActiveCampaign or Mailchimp via Zapier.

---

### 4. **Payment Processing Fees Are Standard (Not Discounted)**

Jobber uses Stripe and Square for payments. You pay standard rates:
- **2.9% + $0.30 per transaction** (card-present)
- **3.4% + $0.30 per transaction** (card-not-present)

Some competitors (like ServiceTitan) negotiate lower rates for high-volume users. Jobber doesn't.

**Reality:** If you're processing $50K/month, you're paying ~$1,500/mo in fees. That's industry-standard, but not discounted.

---

### 5. **Not Ideal for Large Operations ($1M+/Year)**

If you're doing $1M+/year with 10+ trucks, Jobber starts feeling limiting. You'll want ServiceTitan's advanced reporting, call tracking, and marketing attribution tools.

**Use case mismatch:**
- Under $500K/year ‚Üí Jobber is perfect
- $500K-$1M ‚Üí Jobber works, but you'll start eyeing ServiceTitan
- Over $1M ‚Üí Upgrade to ServiceTitan
