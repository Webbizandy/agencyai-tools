### The Standard Sales Pipeline

**Typical Setup:**
- Stage 1: **Lead** (new prospect, not qualified yet)
- Stage 2: **Qualified** (confirmed budget, need, timeline)
- Stage 3: **Proposal Sent** (pitch delivered, waiting for decision)
- Stage 4: **Negotiation** (they're interested, working out details)
- Stage 5: **Closed Won** (deal signed, money incoming)

**Activity Rules:**
- Lead → Qualified: Discovery call scheduled within 48 hours
- Qualified → Proposal: Proposal sent within 7 days
- Proposal → Negotiation: Follow-up call within 3 days
- Negotiation → Closed: Decision deadline set

- End of day: Reps schedule tomorrow's activities

## FAQs

**Q: Can I really run an agency on the $14/month plan?**  
A: No. You'll be manually logging emails, setting reminders by hand, and building reports in spreadsheets. Go straight to Advanced ($29) or Professional ($49) if you want automation and email sync.

**Q: How long does it take to set up Pipedrive?**  
A: 2-3 hours for basic setup (import contacts, build pipeline stages, connect email). Add another 2-4 hours for automation, custom fields, and team training. Most agencies are fully operational within one week.

**Q: What happens if we outgrow Pipedrive?**  
A: You can upgrade to Power ($64) or Enterprise ($99) plans. If you need enterprise-grade features (complex permissions, advanced security), you'll eventually migrate to Salesforce or HubSpot Enterprise. Most agencies stay on Pipedrive until they hit 50+ employees.

**Q: Does Pipedrive integrate with [insert tool here]?**  
A: Yes, probably. Native integrations: Gmail, Outlook, Slack, QuickBooks, Xero, Mailchimp, Zapier (which connects to 5,000+ other tools). Check their integrations page before buying.

**Q: Can we import our existing CRM data?**  
A: Yes. Pipedrive has import wizards for CSV files and direct migrations from Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, and others. Expect some manual cleanup (duplicate contacts, messy fields). Budget 4-8 hours for a clean import.

**Q: What's the cancellation policy?**  
A: Month-to-month plans: Cancel anytime, no penalty. Annual plans: No refunds on unused months. Start monthly, switch to annual after you're sure it works.

## Limitations You Need to Know

1. **Essential Plan is Useless for Teams:** No automation, no email tracking, no custom reporting. It's a trial disguised as a paid plan. Skip it.

2. **Email Campaigns Require Add-On:** Pipedrive's built-in email marketing (Campaigns) costs extra ($16/user/mo). If you need email sequences, integrate Mailchimp or ActiveCampaign instead.

3. **Learning Curve for Automation:** Workflow automation is powerful but not intuitive. Expect to watch tutorials and experiment. First automation takes 30-45 minutes to build. After that, 5-10 minutes per workflow.

4. **Limited Marketing Features:** Pipedrive is built for sales, not marketing. No landing pages, no lead scoring, no ad integrations. If you need full marketing automation, add HubSpot or ActiveCampaign.

5. **Reporting Requires Professional Plan:** Basic reports come with Advanced, but custom dashboards and revenue forecasting require Professional ($49/user). If you need real reporting, don't cheap out.
