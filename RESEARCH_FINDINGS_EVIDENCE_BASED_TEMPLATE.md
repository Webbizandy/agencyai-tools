- Second touch: Newsletter signup

- Fourth touch: Product offer
- Fifth touch: Affiliate promotion

### Pattern #5: CONTENT DEPTH = AUTHORITY
- 2,500-5,000 words minimum
- 10-20 screenshots
- 8-12 FAQ questions
- 3-5 use case examples
- Comparison to 3-4 alternatives
**Evidence:** Top-ranking pages average 3,200 words (Backlinko, Chatimize)

### Anti-Pattern #1: Shallow Content
**Bad:** AI Tool Guru model (300 words, one screenshot)

**Fix:** Minimum 2,500 words, 10+ screenshots

**Bad:** Affiliate links everywhere, no owned capture
**Why it fails:** No list building, no recurring revenue
**Fix:** Email capture as primary, affiliate as secondary

**Bad:** "Best AI tools" (broad, competitive)

### Anti-Pattern #5: Missing Social Proof
**Bad:** Just your opinion, no data
**Why it fails:** Low trust, low conversion
