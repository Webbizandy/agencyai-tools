Affordable CRM with everything you need. Sales automation, email integration, and reporting without the enterprise price tag.

What It Does

What it does: Full-featured CRM that tracks contacts, deals, and sales activities. Automation, email integration, and reporting all included.

Why it matters: You get 90% of Salesforce's features for 10% of the price. If you need a real CRM but don't have enterprise budget, this is the move.

Who it's for: Small to mid-size agencies managing client relationships, consultants who need more than HubSpot's free tier, or anyone who wants powerful CRM without breaking the bank.

Andy's notes: Zoho is underrated. It's not sexy, but it works. The UI feels a bit dated, but once you get past that, it's solid. Free tier supports 3 users, which is perfect for small teams. Paid plans are cheap compared to Salesforce or HubSpot. Only downside? The learning curve is steeper than HubSpot.
