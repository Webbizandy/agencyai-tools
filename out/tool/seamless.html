What It Does

Need to call the VP of Marketing at a Fortune 500 company? Seamless gives you their direct dial in 10 seconds.

Real-time contact data — emails, phone numbers, job titles, company info. And it's constantly updated, so you're not calling dead numbers or bouncing emails.

I use this for high-ticket outbound when I need to call prospects directly. Way better than Apollo or Hunter for phone numbers.

Free tier gives you 50 credits/month. Paid plans start at $147/month, which is pricey but worth it if you're closing big deals.
