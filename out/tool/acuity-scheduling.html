Scheduling software for service businesses. Appointments, payments, and reminders all automated.

What It Does

**What it does:** Online scheduling platform that lets clients book appointments, pay upfront, and get automated reminders. Syncs with your calendar and handles time zones automatically.

**Why it matters:** If you're selling time (coaching calls, strategy sessions, consultations), you need a way for people to book without the back-and-forth. Acuity handles it all — booking, payment, reminders, and rescheduling.

**Who it's for:** Coaches and consultants selling sessions, agencies offering discovery calls, or anyone who needs clients to book and pay in one step.

**Andy's notes:** Acuity is more powerful than Calendly but also more complex. If you need payment integration, intake forms, and package booking, this is the one. If you just need simple scheduling, Calendly is easier. The Squarespace acquisition hasn't changed much — it still works great as a standalone tool.
