CRM built for Google Workspace. Lives inside Gmail, syncs with Google Calendar, and feels native to Google.

What It Does

**What it does:** CRM designed specifically for Google Workspace users. Integrates directly with Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Drive.

**Why it matters:** If you live in Gmail (and most people do), switching to a separate CRM is painful. Copper lives inside Gmail — you can manage contacts, deals, and tasks without leaving your inbox.

**Who it's for:** Agencies using Google Workspace, consultants who live in Gmail, or anyone who wants a CRM that feels native to Google.

**Andy's notes:** The Gmail integration is seamless. You can see contact history, log notes, and update deals right from your inbox. The downside? It only works well if you're all-in on Google. If you use Outlook or other tools, this isn't for you. Pricing is competitive with Pipedrive but you get better Google integration.
