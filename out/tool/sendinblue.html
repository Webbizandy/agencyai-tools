What It Does

**What it does:** All-in-one marketing platform with email, SMS, live chat, and CRM. Unlimited contacts on all plans — you only pay for emails sent.

**Why it matters:** Most platforms charge based on list size. Brevo charges based on emails sent. If you have a big list but only email them once a month, this saves you a fortune.

**Who it's for:** Agencies managing multiple client lists, consultants with large but inactive lists, or anyone who wants email, SMS, and chat in one platform.

**Andy's notes:** The unlimited contacts model is genius if you have a big list. I've seen people save $500+/month switching from Mailchimp to Brevo. The SMS and live chat features are solid bonuses. Only downside? The automation builder isn't as advanced as ActiveCampaign or ConvertKit.
